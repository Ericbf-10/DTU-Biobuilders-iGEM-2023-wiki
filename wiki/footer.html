Since its inception in 2009 in Denmark, the DTU Biobuilders, primarily driven by students and guided by faculty supervisors, have consistently excelled in the iGEM competition, securing numerous awards including gold medals and special recognitions in various categories, and contributing 102 novel BioBricks to the scientific community.
