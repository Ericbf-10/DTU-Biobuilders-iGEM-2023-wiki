Sustainable Development Goals

For this years iGEM project, we hope to uphold and push forward three of the UN's Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). These include Clean Water and Sanitation, Good Health and Well-being, and Life Below Water.

Through developing our innovative biosensor, we hope to promote a high turnover effort in PFAS screening in Denmark and across the world. By making our device rapid and low-cost, we hope to help make widespread environmental screening more accesible, helping to keep our water, our wildlife, and ultimatley our health safe.
